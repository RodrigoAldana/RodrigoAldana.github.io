Hi! I am Rodrigo Aldana-López.

I am currently a Ph.D. student at the University of Zaragoza, studying the effects and scheduling of perception latency in robotic systems with multiple agents. My interests lie in the intersection between learning, control and information theory. Previously, I worked at the multi-agent autonomous systems laboratory at Intel Labs. Feel free to contact me about any of my work, or if you want to discuss something new!
