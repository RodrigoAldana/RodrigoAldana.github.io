Pendulum example

We consider the system \[\dot{x}_1 = x_2,\qquad \dot{x}_2 = -a\sin x_1 - b x_2.\]

The animation shows trajectories for different initial conditions. Damping \(b>0\) produces decay of energy, so trajectories spiral toward an equilibrium modulo the \(2\pi\) periodicity of the angle.
