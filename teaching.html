Teaching



Western Institute of Technology and Higher Education (ITESO)


Computer architecture (Undergraduate level). Principal instructor: This course covers an overview of basic concepts of computer organization and architecture, ranging from some history, the power wall, some instruction set architectures, critical routes, pipeline, cache memory among other topics. The students use Verilog to develop a copy of the classical pipelined MIPS processor and run a Hanoi tower program on it using MIPS assembly.

Monterrey Institute of Technology and Higher Education (ITESM)


Topics on advanced control with application to the control of Aerial Robots (Undergraduate level). Assistant/technical instructor (~20% of the course): This course covers basic nonlinear control theory, mostly applied to quadrotors.
